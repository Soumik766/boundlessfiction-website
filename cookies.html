Cookie Policy

This website doesn't use cookies.

Last updated: 13 July 2026

boundlessfiction.com sets no cookies. There are no analytics cookies, no advertising cookies, no tracking pixels and no third-party trackers on this website. Because of that, you won't see a cookie consent banner here — there is nothing to consent to.

One thing is stored on your device: when you switch the site's language between English and Bangla, we save that choice in your browser's local storage (a single entry named bf-lang) so the site remembers your preference next time. This value never leaves your browser, is not sent to our servers, and contains nothing personal. You can remove it at any time by clearing your browser's site data for boundlessfiction.com.

Third-party services this site loads: our fonts are served by Google Fonts, which delivers font files without setting cookies. Links to Google Play open the Play Store, which is governed by Google's own policies once you leave our site.

The Boundless Fiction app is covered separately — how the app handles your account and data is described in our Privacy Policy.

Questions? Write to contact@boundlessfiction.com.
