Boundless Fiction Account Deletion

Users may request deletion of their Boundless Fiction account and associated personal data at any time.

How to Request Account Deletion

In-App

  1. Open Boundless Fiction.
  2. Go to Profile → Account Security → Delete Account.
  3. Follow the on-screen instructions.

By Email

If you cannot access your account, send a deletion request to:

boundlessfictionapp@gmail.com

Please include the email address associated with your account.

What Data Will Be Deleted

Upon approval of your deletion request, we will permanently delete:

  • Account profile information
  • Email address and authentication records
  • Reading history
  • Comments and reviews
  • User preferences
  • Notifications and settings

What Data May Be Retained

The following information may be retained for a limited period where required for security, fraud prevention, legal compliance, dispute resolution, or backup purposes:

  • Server logs
  • Security records
  • Backup archives

Retention Period

Most personal data is deleted immediately or within 30 days of processing the request.

Backup copies may remain for up to 90 days before being permanently removed.

Contact

For account deletion requests or questions, contact:

boundlessfictionapp@gmail.com
