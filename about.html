About

A publishing house in every pocket.

Bangladesh is full of storytellers — artists sketching panels in notebooks, writers filling drafts no one ever reads, readers who grew up on borrowed comics. What it never had was a digital home of its own: a platform where Bengali visual stories are first-class, not an afterthought in someone else's catalogue.

Boundless Fiction is that home. It's the first digital visual-content app in Bangladesh built for Bengalis — a mobile platform where readers discover original manga, comics and novels in Bangla and English, and where creators publish their own series with nothing standing between them and their audience.

Readers get a fast, beautiful reading experience tuned for real life here — light on data, generous with caching, comfortable in every reading mode. Creators get a studio in their pocket: chapters, covers, scheduled releases, reviews, followers and rankings.

We're starting in Bangladesh, with Bangladeshi creators at the centre — because the next great manga doesn't have to come from anywhere else. It can start here.
