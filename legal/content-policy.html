Content Policy & Community Guidelines

Last updated: May 2026

Boundless Fiction is a platform for manga, manhua, comics, and stories. We want to foster a creative, respectful community. These guidelines apply to all content uploaded by creators and all interactions by users.

1. Allowed Content ✅

  • Original manga, manhua, comics, and written stories
  • Fan works that are clearly labeled as fan-made and non-commercial
  • Content in any language
  • Mature/adult content (18+) that is properly tagged
  • Violence in narrative context that is appropriate to the story genre
  • Fictional exploration of difficult themes (war, trauma, etc.) handled responsibly

2. Prohibited Content ❌

  • Child Sexual Abuse Material (CSAM): Any content that sexualizes minors is strictly prohibited and will be reported to authorities immediately. Zero tolerance.
  • Copyright infringement: Uploading content you do not own or have rights to without proper licensing
  • Hate speech: Content that promotes violence or discrimination based on race, religion, gender, sexual orientation, disability, or nationality
  • Real person fiction (RPF) of a sexual nature: Sexual content involving real, identifiable people without their consent
  • Doxxing: Publishing private information about real people without consent
  • Spam and scams: Deceptive content, phishing, or unsolicited advertising
  • Malware: Content containing malicious code or links
  • Impersonation: Pretending to be another creator, celebrity, or Boundless Fiction staff

3. Mature Content Rules

Mature (18+) content is allowed under these conditions:

  • Must be tagged as "Mature" or "18+" when publishing
  • Must not involve minors in sexual situations under any circumstances, including fictional characters who appear or are stated to be under 18
  • Extreme graphic violence outside of narrative context must be tagged

4. Intellectual Property

You must have the rights to all content you upload. Fan works are allowed if clearly labeled as non-commercial fan creations. Uploading scanlations or translations of licensed works without authorization may result in DMCA takedowns.

5. Enforcement

Violations of this policy may result in:

  • Warning: First minor violation — content removed, warning issued
  • Strike: Repeated violations — content removed, temporary upload restriction
  • Suspension: Serious violations — temporary account suspension
  • Permanent ban: Severe violations (CSAM, repeated infringement) — immediate permanent ban and report to authorities where applicable

6. Reporting Violations

Use the in-app report button on any content or profile to report violations. Our moderation team reviews all reports.

For urgent matters: boundlessfictionapp@gmail.com

7. Appeals

If you believe your content was removed in error, contact us at boundlessfictionapp@gmail.com with your username and a description of the content removed.
